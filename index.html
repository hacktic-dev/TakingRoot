Pitch

Taking Root is a cozy game about caring for houseplants in real time and watching them thrive.

Description

Taking Root is a cozy game in which you can purchase many different species of houseplants and care for them in your home. Every plant has individual light and watering requirements, meaning the game teaches you how to care for all different types of plants in a realistic simulation. Plants grow in real time, so you can recreate the joy of watching your plants grow from a small cutting to a mature plant, and seeing your living space come to life.

Using the money you make by selling cuttings of your plants, you can buy different furniture items, which can be customised however you like, in order to decorate your space exactly the way you like it.
